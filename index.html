Hi, I'm Kristen Jaquez.

I'm Kristen Jaquez — a former teacher with over 20 years in classrooms spanning kindergarten all the way through 12th grade. I've taught everything from early literacy to computer science, which means I've seen firsthand what works, what doesn't, and what teachers actually need.

Today I work in EdTech as a professional development specialist, helping educators navigate new tools and technologies. And I'll be honest — even I feel overwhelmed with AI at times. The updates never stop, the tools multiply weekly, and the noise is deafening.

So I built the resource I wished existed — for teachers, for homeschool families, and for anyone else who just wants to keep up without burning out. The Guiding Thread is my way of being the trusted neighbor who explains what it all actually means for your life.

You don't need to understand everything about AI. You just need the right thread to follow.
